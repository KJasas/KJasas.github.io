Twitter

The goal was to compare companies stock prices fluctuations versus the opinion about the company.
Let's say we are interested in "Apple". First of all, using Twitter API we extract 1000-2000 tweets of the day containing words "Apple", "Iphone", "Ipad", etc. Then we eliminate all the repeated tweets (known as retweets) and are left with around 3-4 hundreds of tweets. We also save every tweets retweet count, and use it a measure of tweets "strength". Next we evaluate the message of the tweet - we look for positive or negative words (we have predefined database of adjectives). Multiplying the score of a tweet by its strength we get final tweets opinion. Finally we sum up all the final scores and get the score of the day. Repeating the process for a month we can compare the fluctuation of opinions with fluctuation of companies stock prices. The stock price data also was extracted automatically from Yahoo Finance.
